My Salkantay Trek Experience

The Salkantay Trek is a 5-day journey through the Andes Mountains in Peru. It offers breathtaking scenery, from snowy peaks to lush jungles.

Each day brings a new challenge, but also a new reward. The altitude, terrain, and distance test your endurance, but the views make it worth it.

Reaching Machu Picchu at the end of the trek is an unforgettable moment.

Typical Trek Itinerary:

  1. Day 1: Start trek and camp.
  2. Day 2: Reach Salkantay Pass.
  3. Day 3: Enter jungle region.
  4. Day 4: Complete the 46-mile trek.
  5. Day 5: Explore Machu Picchu.
