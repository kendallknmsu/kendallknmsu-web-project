Welcome to My Salkantay Trek Journey

This website is about my dream hike through the Salkantay Trek in Peru. The Salkantay Trek is one of the most beautiful alternative routes to Machu Picchu.

I created this site to document the experience, share tips, and inspire others to take on this unforgettable adventure.

Why I Chose This Trek:

  • Less crowded than the Inca Trail
  • Beautiful mountain views
  • Challenging but rewarding
